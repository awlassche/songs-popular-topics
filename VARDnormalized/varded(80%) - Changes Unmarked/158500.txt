
    
      
        HAbje ien gebursten hand?
        Habje noes of oer verbrand?
        Habje die krauwoogje?
        Spiel moor vooke smier, smier, smier,
        Hat geniest dan bold wier,
        Hups doer mien Pottoogje.
      
      
        Moor schold igh noe sjonghen oet,
        Mat doer Salf, on Smier, on Kroedt,
        Wie al vurdiel krieghen,
        Igh schold sjonghen alsoe lanck,
        Dat igh sulf wierd sijck on bank,
        Doorum would igh swieghen.
